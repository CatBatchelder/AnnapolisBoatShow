ABOUT

Set in the seaport of Annapolis, we displays catamarans, mono hulls, racing boats, and inflatables - a total of over 100 new and brokerage boats. Check out the vessels and spend some time shopping for the latest equipment, including electronics, clothing, and other accessories. Attend Seminars, small concerts, venders, and jump on to hundred of boats for a weekend only Annapolis can provide.

Hosted by the same people that host the United States Sailboat Show and the United States Powerboat Show

BOATING SEMINARS

Free boating seminars are offered daily by the Chesapeake Bay Magazine. Some of the topics discussed will include: seamenship, navigation, piloting, cruise planning, engine maintenance, marine elctronics, and much more.

ENTERTAINMENT

A variety of entertainment is offered accross the entire weekend. From music, dancing, prizes, beer and wine tastings and so much more. Take a breather from the sailboats and visit our Entertainment Plaza and enjoy continuous entertainment.
